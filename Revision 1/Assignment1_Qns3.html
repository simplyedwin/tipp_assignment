How to make the Awesomely Chocolate Cake!

Here we teach you how to make the awesomely chocolate with the right ingredients and steps. Isn't it sound AWESOME?

So what are you waiting for ? Lets GO!!!

Ingredients needed:

  • Chocolate chips
  • Kit Kate
  • Sneaker Choco Bar
  • Mars Choco Bar
  • Flour
  • Water

Steps for the awesome cake in the world:

  1. Beat the chocolate chips, kit kate, Sneaker Choco bar and Mars Choco bar together
  2. Add water every 10sec while beating the mixture in step 1
  3. Add Flour
  4. Add remaining water
  5. Put in toaster to bake for 30 min
  6. Put some topping if you want

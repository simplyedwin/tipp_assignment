So Shiok Otah Bun

The So Shiok Otah Bun is so shiok that the soft, fluffy bread, on top of which lies embedded strips of damn good spicy otah will melt in your mouth! Not overly fiery, it's paired with a cool mayonnaise dressing and cheese. A true Modern Singaporean classic bun.
